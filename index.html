What we do

We develop advanced digitizers and data acquisition systems—engineered for compactness, and affordability. From prototyping to deployment, we support you all the way.

LiDAR DAQ

High-speed, low-latency LiDAR acquisition systems. Fully tested and integration-ready for your sensing stack.

RADAR DAQ

Latest development for accurate timing and clean signal paths for RADAR acquisition. Our systems are built with precision synchronization in mind.

Custom FPGA DAQ and SDR

We build flexible, high-throughput FPGA systems for your custom applications. Whether you're capturing RF signals or sensor data, we deliver low latency, real-time processing, and seamless integration with your system architecture.

Product Development

End-to-end electronic product development—from idea to prototype to production. We handle analog, digital, and embedded design under one roof.
